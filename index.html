About SereniTea Café

Located in Serenity Garden, Mzuzu City, SereniTea Café offers a calm and welcoming environment where handcrafted drinks, fresh smoothies, and herbal teas meet a relaxing ambience. Join us for coffee, tea, or a vibrant social evening with live events.

Herbal Drinks

Coffee & Tea

Events

Join us for Dinner & Jazz Nights in a cozy and serene environment. Our events are a perfect way to enjoy live music and great drinks with friends.
